About the bot

What KADIYA MD does

👁️

Auto status view

ANU MD quietly views your contacts' status updates for you, the moment they're posted.

🔷

Auto status react

Reacts to statuses with a set of emojis you choose, so you're never left on "seen" only.

🛡️

Flexible reply modes

Switch between public, private, inbox-only, or groups-only — your bot, your rules.

🔁

Stays online

Automatic reconnect if your session drops, so the bot keeps working without you checking in.

🔐

Private by number

Each WhatsApp number gets its own settings, locked behind a password only you receive.

📰

Newsletter tools

Follows and reacts to channels automatically, keeping your presence active across WhatsApp.
